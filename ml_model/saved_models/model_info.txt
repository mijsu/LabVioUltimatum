LabVio Unified Health Risk Prediction Model
========================================================

Single Model for All Lab Types:
  - Training samples: 10,000 (CBC + Urinalysis + Lipid)
  - Gradient Boosting Accuracy: 1.000
  - Logistic Regression Accuracy: 1.000

Features (19):
  - lab_type: Lab type identifier (0=CBC, 1=Urinalysis, 2=Lipid)
  - CBC: WBC, RBC, Hemoglobin, Platelets
  - Lipid: Cholesterol, HDL, LDL, Triglycerides, VLDL
  - Glucose/A1C: Glucose, A1C
  - Urinalysis: pH, Specific Gravity, Protein, Ketones, Blood, Nitrites, Leukocyte Esterase

Target Classes (3):
  - 0: Low Risk
  - 1: Moderate Risk
  - 2: High Risk
